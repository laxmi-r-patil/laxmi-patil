About Me

Crafting visual
narratives

I'm Laxmi Patil, a freelance photographer based in Gulbarga, Karnataka. With over eight years behind the lens, I specialise in portrait, documentary, and nature photography — finding the extraordinary in ordinary light.

My work is rooted in stillness. The most compelling images emerge not from chasing action, but from waiting for the world to reveal itself — one honest frame at a time.
